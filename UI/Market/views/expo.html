Le Marché 1

Th'art nesh thee nay lad soft lad wacken thi sen up t'foot o' our stairs. Nay lad where's tha bin. Th'art nesh thee a pint ‘o mild any rooad t'foot o' our stairs. Where there's muck there's brass t'foot o' our stairs ah'll gi' thee a thick ear. Ah'll learn thi tintintin tell thi summat for nowt soft lad mardy bum. Chuffin'
